Syndicate

Syndika is a top-notch Web3 syndicate that unites Executive Members, Syndicate Members and Associates, Team members, Advisors, and Partners to collaborate and facilitate Web3 startups, corporates, and initiatives in their growth.

Executive Members

Executive Members work in the syndicate, and are in charge of the general management, the technological management, and the business management of the syndicate.

Team

Syndika's team members work in the syndicate. Currently, in Syndika's companies, there are 80+ team members, offering technology consulting, architecture and development, and Web3 economics and data science.
